Objective

The main goal is to score more points than the opposing team by shooting the ball through the opponentâ€™s hoop.

Players and Teams

Each team has five players on the court at any time. Substitutions can be made as often as needed.

Scoring

  1. A field goal (basket) inside the three-point line is worth 2 points.
  2. A basket made from beyond the three-point line is worth 3 points.
  3. Free throws are worth 1 point each.
