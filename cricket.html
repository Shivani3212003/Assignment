Cricket

Objective

The main goal is to score more runs than the opposing team.

Players and Teams

  1. Each team consists of 11 players.
  2. Players have different roles: batsmen, bowlers, fielders, and a wicketkeeper.

Equipment

  1. Bat: Made of wood, flat on one side.
  2. Ball: Hard and covered in leather.
  3. Wickets: Each set consists of three stumps topped by two bails.
  4. Protective Gear: Includes pads, gloves, helmets, and guards.
